How far will you go to fulfill your dreams?
How much can you endure?
How long will you wait?
Will you be same enough, when you achieve it?

These questions stare into the face of Mith Desai, who is unable to find venue to screen his first film. Raja, his buddy, suggest that they screen the film by organizing film festival in their village. This proves to be bigger challenge than expected...

The challenges come in different forms -
Senile uncle, power shortage, demented curator, a projector that hates art films, a gay sponsor, and above all illiterate villagers.
