Synopsis:

Southland Tales is an ensemble piece set in the futuristic landscape of Los Angeles 

on July 4, 2008, as it stands on the brink of social, economic and environmental 

disaster. Boxer Santaros is an action star who's stricken with amnesia. His life 

intertwines with Krysta Now, an adult film star developing her own reality television 

project, and Ronald Taverner, a Hermosa Beach police officer who holds the key to a 

vast conspiracy.
