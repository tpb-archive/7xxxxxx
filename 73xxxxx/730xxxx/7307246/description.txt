Poor Xander is down on his luck so he calls on his rich sibling for a place to stay 'til he can get back on his feet. His sibling's a bit of a prick though and is somehow married to the perfectly stacked Blake Rose; a loveless and sexless marriage, one might point out. Blake would love to help the wayward Xander but her husband is disgusted and storms off. So guess what Blake and Xander do while alone in the house...
