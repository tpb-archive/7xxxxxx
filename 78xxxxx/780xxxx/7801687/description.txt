Title: Dracula 3: The Path of the Dragon
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 1st-person
Released: 21st August 2008
Developer: Kheops Studio
Publisher: Anuman Interactive
Description:
Dracula 3: The Path of the Dragon is a horror adventure video game based on the characters from Bram Stoker's Dracula. As Father Arno Moriani, you are sent by the Vatican to Transylvania to investigate a candidate for sainthood. But, in the shadows of the ruins that were once the castle of Vlad the Impaler, otherwise known as Count Dracula, your investigation takes an unexpected turn... Carry out your inquiry amidst tales of bloody deeds and ancestral legends. Will you survive before fear takes over?

How to play:
1) Burn Dracula3.iso on a DVD or mount it on a virtual drive;
2) Run Setup.exe and install the game (it will start automatically if you have autorun enabled);
3) Play the game without the disc using main executable or shortcuts (DRM free).
