============================================

Sine Mora is a horizontal shoot'em up that provides a unique take on
 challenge, where time is the ultimate factor. Mixing classic shooter
 sensibilities with contemporary presentation, Sine Mora is a gorgeous
 shoot'em up that offers a Story Mode that weaves an over-the-top tale
 and an Arcade Mode that provides deep, satisfying gameplay to challenge
 fans of the genre. With many ways to manipulate time, Sine Mora
 features over 50 weapon combinations to complete each beautiful stage
 that form fits to the player's skills with scaling difficulty
 Soundtrack composed by Akira Yamaoka and featuring boss designs by
 Mahiro Maeda
