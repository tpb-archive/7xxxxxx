One of the most particular railway in Scandinavia and in Europe is surely the 
Kiruna - Narvik section. This line is all situated north of the Polar Circle. 
It's called Ofotbanen in Norway, and Malmbanan in Sweden, is 168 Km lenght and 
is the final section of the "Iron ore line" built to serve Kiruna iron mine, 
one of the world's most important. 

The train drivers view was recorded in July 12, 2002.
