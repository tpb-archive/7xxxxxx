Description:
~~~~~~~~~~~~

Hearts of Iron III: Their Finest Hour puts you back in the commander's seat
during World War II. Win the war with new units, strategies and enhanced game
modes.

Destroy your enemies from within with an expanded espionage system that
includes new missions and covert operations. A new battle plan mode allows you
to load historical plans or draw your own; all of which can be shared with
allies in real-time during multiplayer.

Along with the enhanced gameplay there are two new battle scenarios and a new
custom game mode that lets you jump straight into the action.

Main Features:
* New unique, elite units for each major nation's army - Ghurkhas, Rangers,
  Imperial guards and more
* A deeper naval invasion system with the ability to develop specialized
  landing and assault crafts
* Increased control over strategic warfare with a new lend-lease system for
  lending your industrial might to allies
* Leaders are now able to gain traits and allocating them is more important
  than ever with a new combat tactics system
* New game mode for single and multiplayer. Start with a clean slate and
  instantly produce units, research technologies and affect political
  alignment. Speed up the early years and jump straight into the action
* Two new detailed battle scenarios, the Finnish Winter War and the Spanish
  Civil War


Install:
~~~~~~~
Unrar, mount/burn image & install. This is an addon and it requires both the
original game and the For The Motherland addon installed. The latter hasn't
seen an ISO release as far as we know, but we found that it works with VACEs
release: 072411 Hearts_Of_Iron_III_For_The_Motherland_STANDALONE-VACE
Play the game using hoi3_tfh.exe. Enjoy & buy it if you like it.
