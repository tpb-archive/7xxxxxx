Author   S.C.Stephens

Title   Effortless

Genre   Romance

Published   June 13th 2011

Plot

A lot can happen in a year. You grow, you change, you learn from your mistakes. And Kiera had made a lot of mistakes to learn from. But she had learned, and she was determined to never cause a man pain again, especially the amazing man who currently held her heart. But life offers new challenges for every relationship, and when Kieras love is put to the ultimate test, will it come out the other side unscathed
