FORMATS:
- DjVu: Scanned book with(out) contents. It opens with WinDjView free program.
- PDF: Scanned or OCRed book with(out) contents. It opens with SumatraPDF free program.
- ODT, DOC: Editable book. It opens with LibreOffice free program or Microsoft Office 2010 commercial program.
- 7z: Compressed archive. It opens with 7zip free program.
- JP2, JBG: Jpeg2000 and JBig image. It opens with XnView free program.
- PNG, JPEG: Common image. It opens with everything.
- CHM: Compressed HTML Documentation with contents. It opens natively.
