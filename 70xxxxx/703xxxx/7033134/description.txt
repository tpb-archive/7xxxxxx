Release Description:

The program allows you to add fantastic glows and fog, generate the
effects of rain, snow, lightning and a starry night... You will be able
to create the effect of an old photo, pencil drawing, mosaic, engraving,
book illustration and much more. Photo Effects Studio also includes a
set of unique filters, for example, poker-work, cubism and photomontage.
You will also be able to make your photos more stylish with the help of
frames and masks and also add text and decorations. The program includes
a large collection of classic and modern frames to any taste. They will
add a final touch to your composition.
