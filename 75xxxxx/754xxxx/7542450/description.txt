Description

~ Map coverage: Singapore, Malaysia & Brunei.~
Download Garmin StreetPilot Onboard Singapore/Malaysia with this one-time purchase and give your iPhone® the best in GPS navigation … even when you don’t have cell coverage. Maps reside on your device, so you can look up addresses and points of interest (POI), such as gas stations, restaurants and ATMs without needing wireless coverage. It’s as close to having a Garmin nüvi personal navigator as an iPhone can get.

Enjoy Premium Navigation

Designed exclusively for your iPhone by Garmin, the world leader in GPS navigation, the Garmin StreetPilot Onboard navigation application turns your Apple device into a premium GPS navigator with turn-by-turn voice-prompted directions that speaks street names and automatically calculates a new route if you get off track.

Garmin StreetPilot Onboard navigation offers rich mapping and many advanced features, but is still simple and intuitive to use. It includes several preloaded POI, PhotoReal™ Junction View, 3D buildings and more.

Features
* Preloaded Navteq and Malsing map. Map coverage: Singapore, Malaysia & Brunei.
* FREE lifetime map update¹.
* Industry-leading Garmin user interface. 
* Voice-prompted, turn-by-turn directions including spoken street names, Text-to-speech (TTS.)
* Millions of preloaded points of interest including gas stations, restaurants and other businesses.
* 3D buildings and landmarks. 
* Lane assist with PhotoReal™ Junction View provides a photo-realistic illustration of the correct lane for different maneuvers. 
* Speed limits for most major roads.
* Where Am I? Emergency locator shows your exact latitude and longitude, the nearest address and intersection, and the closest hospitals, police stations and gas stations. 
* Exit services allow you to quickly see services such as restaurants, gas stations and accommodation available at exits along your route. 
* Trip planner allows you to create a route with multiple stops in advance with automatic or manual ordering of destinations.
* Weather feature shows current conditions and a 5-day forecast.
* Location sharing via Facebook or SMS.

** Notice: Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

¹ FREE lifetime map updates entitle you to receive up to 4 map data updates per year, when and as such updates are made available on the App Store, for this specific Garmin product only until this product’s useful life expires or Garmin no longer receives map data from its third party supplier, whichever is shorter.
